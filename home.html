Who Is KSI?

Hey there! I'm KSI, but you can call me JJ. I'm a British content creator, rapper, and boxer, born on June 19, 1993, in Watford, Hertfordshire, England. My journey in the digital world kicked off with gaming content on YouTube, where I showcased my skills and humor through FIFA gameplay videos. Over the years, my channel has evolved into a hub of entertainment, featuring vlogs, music, and collaborations with some amazing folks. Speaking of music, you might have heard some of my tracks like "Lamborghini," "Friends with Benefits," and "Down Like That." I like to keep things diverse, so you'll find a bit of everything on my channel. Beyond the screen, I've dipped my toes into the boxing ring, taking on some high-profile matches against fellow YouTubers. The bouts against Joe Weller and Logan Paul were definitely intense, and the experiences taught me a lot about discipline and pushing my limits.
